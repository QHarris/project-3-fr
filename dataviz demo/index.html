My Week of Getting Bit

Since 2018, the Department of Health and Human Hygiene has collected dog bite data from reported incidents around the city. According to the NYC Health Code, dog bites from any animal are required to be reported within 24 hours of the event. This helps the Health Department follow up with victims to avoid unecessary rabies shots.
